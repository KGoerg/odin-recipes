Creamy Beef Gnocchi

(Image sourced from Unsplash)

Description

This recipe is a slight alteration of Cooking in the Midwest's "Creamy Beef and Gnocchi" recipe. I've changed a couple of things, otherwise this is practically the same recipe. Give him a shout out!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 1/2 TBSP of dehydrated minced onion
  • 1/2 tsp each salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp each of paprika and italian seasoning
  • 1 TBSP of minced garlic. I tend to use the dehydrated stuff
  • 1 handful of spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 packs of gnocchi

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and minced onion over medium-high heat.
  2. Drain a majority of the grease. I find leaving some helps keep the ground beef juicy and flavorful.
  3. Season meat with salt, pepper, paprika, and italian seasoning.
  4. Add minced garlic, spinach leaves, beef broth, heavy cream, parmesan cheese, and gnocchi.
  5. Bring to a boil and lower heat to medium-low. Cover and let cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Remove the lid, stir well, and cook for a few more minutes or until gnocchi is cooked through. Depending on how cooked the gnocchi is, you may want to cover and let simmer for a few more minutes instead.
  7. Serve and top with more parmesan cheese.
